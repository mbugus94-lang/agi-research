### Research-Informed Design

**Miller's Law**: Working memory capacity limited to 7±2 items
- Implementation: `WORKING_MEMORY_CAPACITY = 7`
- Auto-consolidation to episodic when capacity exceeded

**Episodic Memory for Learning**:
- Task execution logs stored as experiences
- Enables retrieval of similar past tasks for planning
- Foundation for test-time adaptation

**Memory-Aware Planning**:
- `_generate_plan()` retrieves relevant past experiences
- Plans include count of relevant past experiences
